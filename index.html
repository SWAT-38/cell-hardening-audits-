📖 User Guide — How to Use This App ▼ Expand ▲ Collapse

This app tracks FLIB cell hardening inspections from first audit through final acceptance. Follow the workflow below in order.

1

📋 Create a New Audit

Click + New Audit to begin a cell hardening inspection. Select the DC, cell number, and inspector name. Work through each checklist item — mark pass or fail and add notes on any failures. An audit should always be performed before opening action items.

2

⚠️ Open Action Items for Failures

After completing an audit, navigate to the Action Items page and create an action item for each failure found. Fill in the Action Item type, DC, Cell, Driveway, Location, Priority, and assign it to the responsible party (Symbotic, Honeywell, or Maintenance). Action items can be opened independently at any time, but should always reference a completed audit.

3

🔄 Track & Close Action Items

Monitor open items on the Action Items page. Update the status to In Progress when work begins and Closed once resolved. Add resolution notes and a resolution photo when closing. Use the priority and DC filters to focus on what needs attention first.

4

📦 Archive the Audit

Once a cell has been accepted by the Walmart Automation Site Engineer, move the audit to the Archive page. All audits — both passing and failing — should be archived after acceptance. The archive serves as the permanent record of the cell’s hardening history.

5

📧 Export & Send PDFs to Symbotic

Use the Export PDF or Email PDF buttons on the Action Items page to generate and send reports directly to Symbotic. PDFs capture the full action item list including status, priority, ticket numbers, and resolution notes — keeping communication clear and documented.

Quick Reference Workflow

📋 New Audit → ⚠️ Action Items → 🔄 Resolve & Close → ✅ Site Engineer Accepts → 📦 Archive
